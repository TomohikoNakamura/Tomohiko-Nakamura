Timbre Replacement of Harmonic and Drum components of Music Audio Signals


Contributors: Tomohiko Nakamura, Hirokazu Kameoka (The University of Tokyo), Kazuyoshi Yoshii and Masataka Goto (National Insitute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology).



This system allows us to replace the frequency characteristics of the harmonic component and the drum timbres of a musical piece with those of another musical piece.

Demo audios

  • All the audio files are from RWC music database.
  • Audio signals of the harmonic and percussive components are normalized such that their maximum power are unity.
