Demonstration of pitch tranposition

To transpose all pitches of music clips, we first computed complex CQT spectrograms of the clips, simply shifted the components of the magnitude CQT spectrograms up or downwards, added phase to them, and finally converted the pitch-transposed CQT spectrogram into audio signals.
