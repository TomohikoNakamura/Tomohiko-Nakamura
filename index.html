Tomohiko Nakamura

I received B.E., M.S. and Ph.D degrees from the University of Tokyo, Japan, in 2011, 2013 and 2016.
I am currently with Intelligent Systems Laboratory, SECOM.
E-mail: Tomohiko.Nakamura.JP[at]ieee.org (replace [at] with "@")

Member of IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS), Information Processing Society of Japan (IPSJ) and Acoustic Society of Japan (ASJ).

Research Interests

  • Audio Signal Synthesis/Construction
    • Fast Signal Reconstruction from Magnitude Spectrogram of Constant-Q transform (or Continuous Wavelet Transform) based on Spectrogram Consistency
      [Demo Page]
  • Music Audio Signal Processing
    • Harmonic-Temporal Factor Decomposition for Monural Source Separation
      [Demo Page]
    • Timbre Replacement of Harmonic and Drum Components for Music Audio Signals
      [Demo Page]
    • Acoustic score following and acousitc automatic accompaniment for musical performance in practice
      [Demo Page]
    • MIDI score following and automatic accompaniment system for polyphonic MIDI input/output
      [Project Page]
    • Multipitch estimation of acoustic signals of music.
      [.pdf]
  • Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition
    • Nonparametric Bayesian inference for signal processing
  • System Biology
    • Local stability/instability analysis of quorum-sensing networks in a multi-cell environment
      [J-Stage]
